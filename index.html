WELCOME TO HOVE 2023!

The HOVE'2023 workshop is scheduled on April 17, 2023 and will be held at Tianjin, China.

This year HOVE will be co-located with DASFAA 2023: The 28th International Conference on Database Systems for Advanced Applications.

High-dimensional vector data management is a fundamental problem in many applications, such as image search, information retrieval, recommendation, social network analysis, and so forth. It has been extensively studied over decades and consists of many topics. Among them the high-dimensional vector similarity search is the most signi cant one, because it serves as the basic functionality in this eld. A commonly adopted setting for high-dimensional vector similarity search is to retrieve the k nearest neighbors (kNN). Over hundreds of algorithms have been proposed to solve the problem from di erent perspectives. How to retrieve a good approximation (high recall) of true kNN with acceptable overhead (low time and space complexity) becomes the most concerned question.With the development of e ective and e cient similarity search methods, topics on top of it become prevalent, such as applying it in distributed or parallel environment. This workshop aims at providing a place for researchers from the elds of database and data engineering to exchange ideas about how to adapt and develop techniques to process, analyze and manage high-dimensional vector data.

The topics of interest include, but are by no means limited to:
  • High-dimensional vector similarity search
  • Maximum inner product search (MIPS) in high-dimensional space
  • Visualization of high-dimensional vector
  • Parallel query processing for high-dimensional vector
  • High-dimensional vector indexing
  • Anomaly detection for high-dimensional vector
  • High-dimensional vector clustering
  • Mining in high-dimensional vector of distributed systems
  • Dimensionality reduction in high-dimensional space
  • High-dimensional vector compression
  • Security and privacy
